About Me

I'm a BS student in Fisheries at Gazipur Agricultural University, passionate about the intersection of biological sciences and computational methods. My academic journey has led me to develop a keen interest in bioinformatics, genomics, and biotechnology.

With a foundation in both biological sciences and programming, I aim to bridge the gap between wet lab experimentation and computational analysis. My research interests span genomics, transcriptomics, and proteomics, with a particular focus on using computational tools to solve real-world problems in fisheries and aquaculture.

As the Vice President of the IT Society at GAU, I foster collaborative environments where technology meets biology, organizing workshops and hackathons that encourage interdisciplinary approaches to scientific challenges.
